About CodeUnity
  • The purpose of this website is to help other programmers who have encountered errors or difficulties in Web, Mobile, and Desktop development. This is a big help to them because here they can post their codes with errors that can be answered or given an answer by other active users of this platform.
  • You can also upload here your completed projects that you want to share with others. Other users can download your project for free to help them practice programming or help them in the the program that they are making.
  • Another feature of codeunity is that you can find programmers here that you can hire to work, you can see their skills and other information about them that will be the basis of their employment.
